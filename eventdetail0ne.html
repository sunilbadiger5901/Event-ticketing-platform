About

GRAMMY®️ and EMMY®️ award-winning rap mogul Curtis “50 Cent” Jackson will tour India as part of his global “The Final Lap Tour 2023” commemorating the 20th anniversary of his debut album ‘Get Rich or Die Tryin’ on November 25th at the DY Patil Stadium in Mumbai. Presented by Tracktical Concerts + VJP, the concert will come to India for one night only and will showcase the best of 50 Cent’s illustrious career with a lineup of fan-favorite and long-awaited tracks. It will also feature supporting performances by globally renowned Indian and international hip-hop & urban acts. Recognised as one of the most talented and prolific music artists of his time, 50 Cent rose to fame with his record-shattering debut album, Get Rich or Die Tryin’. Since then, he has sold more than 30 million albums worldwide and has been awarded the most prestigious accolades. Jackson has leveraged his star power to achieve unparalleled success as an entrepreneur, actor, director, and executive producer. From Get Rich or Die Tryin’ being one of the fastest-selling albums in history to creating one of the most influential deals in hip-hop with the sale of Vitaminwater, Jackson continues to break records. Currently, he holds the record for the highest rated premiere of a series ever on Starz with “Power Book II: Ghost.”
